Indai CPI Inflation Case Study

This project analyzes the Consumer Price Index (CPI) in India to understand inflation patterns, key contributing categories, and the effects of major economic and global events. 
The study uses Excel-based data analysis to identify category-wise contributions, year-on-year (YoY) and month-on-month (MoM) inflation trends, 
and correlations between price changes and external factors like crude oil prices.

üß† Business Objectives

This case study aims to explore and answer the following key analytical questions:
Category Contribution: Identify the contribution of broader categories (Food, Energy, Housing, etc.) to total CPI.
Year-on-Year Inflation Trend: Analyze CPI growth trends from 2017 onward and identify the year with the highest inflation.
Food Inflation Trend: Track food inflation over 12 months ending May 2023 and identify major contributing subcategories.
COVID-19 Impact: Examine how the pandemic affected inflation across essential sectors such as food, healthcare, and transportation.
Oil Price Correlation: Assess the relationship between imported crude oil prices and inflation trends across categories (2021‚Äì2023).
